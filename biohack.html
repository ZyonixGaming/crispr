🧪 Source DNA

📘 How to Use

  1. Paste a full genome sequence into the Source DNA area (or leave empty).
  2. Load a preset (optional) – click "Horse" or "Car" on the right to quickly set up a common gene pool.
  3. Edit the gene pool:
    • 🧪 Source mode – genes automatically use the DNA bases from your pasted sequence.
    • 🧬 Random mode – genes use a custom set of bases (like a preset).
    • Click the 🧪/🧬 icon to toggle a gene's mode, or click base buttons directly to switch to Random and change them.
  4. Save your custom preset (optional) – click “Save current preset” if you want to reuse this gene selection later.
  5. Copy the two flasks: use the 📋 buttons next to Flask 1 and Flask 2.
  6. Paste each flask into a separate flask in‑game.
  7. Drain the biohacker vat (add something first), then add Flask 1 and Flask 2.
  8. Press the red button to start the process.
